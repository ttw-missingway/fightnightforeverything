# Pixel art credits

Stand-in character and player portraits come from the **Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup 32x32
tileset** (crawl-tiles Oct-5-2010), released as **CC0 / public domain**.

Source: https://opengameart.org/content/dungeon-crawl-32x32-tiles

- `chars/` — sprites used as stand-ins for the fighting game's roster
  (includes the humanoid NPC sprites that originally shipped as player art)

Player mugshots in `faces/` are derived from **EverFace** by Efilheim, 18
character portraits released as **CC0 / public domain**
(https://opengameart.org/content/everface). We processed them into a Game
Boy Camera look — 4-shade DMG palette, ordered Bayer dithering, mirrored
variants — with a small script; the derivatives remain CC0.

Stage backgrounds in `stages/` are also **CC0 / public domain**:

- `street.png` — cropped from "Streets of Fight" by ansimuz
  (https://opengameart.org/content/streets-of-fight)
- `dusk_*.png` — "Mountain at Dusk Background" parallax layers
  (https://opengameart.org/content/mountain-at-dusk-background)
- `jungle_*.png` — "Sunny Land 2D Pixel Art Pack" environment layers by ansimuz
  (https://opengameart.org/content/sunny-land-2d-pixel-art-pack)
- `space_*.png` — "Space Background" parallax layers
  (https://opengameart.org/content/space-background-3)

No attribution required (CC0), credited here as a courtesy.
